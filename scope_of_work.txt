## Expected Outcome

A browser-based gaming platform that allows visitors to instantly play games while encouraging long-term engagement through virtual rewards, achievements, daily featured content, personalized recommendations, and weekly leaderboards. Users can enjoy the platform as guests or create accounts to synchronize progress, while administrators maintain full control over games, rewards, categories, and platform content through a centralized dashboard.
